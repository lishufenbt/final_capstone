Car accidents cause severe casualty, we are here to help you predict them

We built up prediction models using the car collision data from 2012-2018 to help you predict the likelihood of an motorist (motor vehicle drivers or passengers) injury in a car crash.

250000 car crashes per year in NYC

Resulted in 50 thousand injuries/deaths per year. Most are injuries/deaths of car drivers and passengers

Multiple attributes affect casualty rate

These attributes include time, date, locations, street conditions, car types, recorded contributing factors by police, weather, etc.

Casualty can be predicted

Predicting the casualty in an accident helps police, hospitals and other stakeholders to better allocate their resources
